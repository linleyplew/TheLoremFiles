What does Lorem Ipsum mean?

Lorem Ipsum is a Latin phase that simply put means "Pain itself".

Lorem Ipsum Breakdown

"Lorem" is the just a piece of the correct Latin word "Dolorem".

"Dolorem" in Latin translates to pain, or sorrow.

"Ipsum" in Latin translates to yourself.

History

It was first used by Cicero in an essay he wrote called, "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum." Or in translation to English, "On the Extremes of Good and Evil"

Purpose in Web Design

Lorem Ipsum's primary purpose is to be used as "filler text." It is used to make sure the designer/developer isnt distracted from the overall layout of the page.
